Wear an N95 mask

If the smog in your city is just too much to handle, consider wearing a properly fitting N95 particulate filter. In extreme situations, a personal filter can make a world of difference.Their material is not designed to filter down the correct micron particle-sized level and they do not seal around the face, leaving large air gaps through which the majority of inhaled air is drawn. Disposable N95 masks, ideally with exhale valves, however, protect the wearer appropriately and should be worn in lieu of surgical masks which have minimal benefit. Surgical masks do not filter at the level necessary to block small particulate matter of 2.5 microns, considered by the W.H.O as the primary element of urban pollution to guard against. you can buy one here

Spider Plant

Snake plant (Sansevieria trifasciata 'Laurentii')
