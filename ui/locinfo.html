Wild linden (Tilia cordata)

A tall variety that is one of the ancestors of the common linden. Lindens are typically planted in cities and gardens. This variety excellently captures CO2 and effectively reduces smog

Bamboo Palm

Reed palm is a common nick name. It can survive in shady indoors. Filters benzene and trichloroethylene and is the good choice to be in places around furniture emitting formaldehyde and filtering trichloroethylene.
