Welcome to the web-app

This app gives an insight about the pollution levels in your locality ...Some precaution methods and lot more.. Use the above tabs to navigate

Our Sensors have collected data from many places ...

These data include .........

    Carbonmonoxide level
    No2 levels
    Methane levels
    pm 10 and pm 2.5 levels
    Temperature,Humidity,location date and time

with these data we would provide you an insight of the pollution levels ,information about what is the major pollutant,alerts if any of the pollution levels croses it limits, a heat map indicating the pollution levels in a map and some preacaution measures to decreaser these pollutants

The system is distributed , mobile and it is collecting data from locations autonomously and the corresponding will be updated

Raw data --> A simple view of the data collected from the senors

Maps --> A map indicating pollution levels

Urban Assitant --> Some precaution measures for urban area

Rural Assitant --> Some precuation measures for rural area
